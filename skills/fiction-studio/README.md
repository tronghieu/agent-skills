# Fiction Studio Skill

**Language / Ngôn ngữ / 语言:** [English](./README.md) | [Tiếng Việt](./README.vi.md) | [中文](./README.zh.md)

This skill transforms your AI agent into a virtual **writers' room** of literary specialists to help you plan, draft, edit, and polish your stories or novels.

## What is Fiction Studio?

Fiction Studio is a structured creative writing suite. Instead of talking to a generic chatbot, you work with a team of specialized AI personalities named after literary masters. Led by **Homer** (the showrunner), each specialist owns a specific aspect of the craft — from plotting with **Aristotle**, building characters with **Fyodor**, creating worlds with **Tolkien**, to writing dialogue with **Oscar** and editing with **Max**.

## Why Use It?

- **Professional Collaboration**: Simulates a real writers' room with specialists focusing on one task at a time (e.g., character psychology before drafting, dialogue polishing after drafting).
- **Interactive Control**: Follows the *Elicitation Rule* — the AI will always pause to offer you numbered choices for your story's direction rather than writing ahead without you.
- **Maintain Consistency**: Includes a consistency checker and a structured `canon.json` to keep track of names, timelines, character traits, and setups/payoffs across long drafts.
- **Creative Roundtables ("Party Mode")**: Call a group brainstorm session with 3–4 specialists to resolve plot holes, discuss endings, or break writer's block.
- **End-to-End Pipeline**: Guides you from a bare 1-sentence idea to a fully compiled manuscript and a book pitch kit.

## The Writing Team

- **Homer**: The showrunner who manages the project, coordinates the team, and maintains files.
- **Aristotle**: Plot Architect (structure, pacing, outlines).
- **Fyodor**: Character Psychologist (wounds, motivations, character arcs, voices).
- **Tolkien**: World Builder (settings, rules, cultures, magic/tech systems).
- **Scheherazade**: Narrative Weaver (scaffold scene lists and write prose drafts).
- **Oscar**: Dialogue Specialist (speech subtext, unique character voices).
- **Max**: Editor (developmental and line editing, revision plans).
- **Virginia**: Beta Reader (gives reader-centric reactions, highlights emotional beats).
- **Borges**: Genre Advisor (conventions and reader expectations).
- **Bloom**: Book Critic (provides high-level literary critiques).

## How to Trigger

Ask your AI agent tasks like:

```text
I have an idea for a sci-fi novel, let's start the Fiction Studio pipeline.
```

```text
My dialogue in this chapter feels dry. Can Oscar take a look?
```

```text
Let's open a Writers' Room session to brainstorm a twist ending for my mystery story.
```
