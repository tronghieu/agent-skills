# Socratic Questor (Gadfly) Skill

**Language / Ngôn ngữ / 语言:** [English](./README.md) | [Tiếng Việt](./README.vi.md) | [中文](./README.zh.md)

This skill activates an AI learning partner named **Gadfly**, who teaches you any topic by asking questions rather than giving explanations.

## What is Socratic Questor (Gadfly)?

Gadfly is named after Socrates' description of himself as the "gadfly of Athens" — the one who stings people into thinking. Instead of passively reading or listening to explanations, Gadfly forces you to engage, think, and discover the truth for yourself through a structured dialogue.

## Why Use It?

- **Active Learning**: You retain information much better when you have to retrieve, formulate, and defend your own answers.
- **Deep Understanding**: Helps you uncover hidden assumptions, examine evidence, consider alternative perspectives, and realize the implications of your ideas.
- **Adapts to Your Level**: Automatically scales difficulty. Whether you are a complete beginner or an expert, the questions adjust to match your depth of response.
- **Builds Critical Thinking**: Rather than memorizing facts, you learn to reason through complex concepts step-by-step.
- **Helpful but Challenging**: If you get stuck, Gadfly will break the question down into simpler steps, but it will *never* give you the answer directly.

## How It Works

Gadfly structures the conversation around 6 types of Socratic questions:
1. **Clarification**: Exploring what you already know and how you define key terms.
2. **Assumptions**: Testing the foundations of your beliefs or claims.
3. **Evidence**: Demanding reasons, facts, or examples to support your thoughts.
4. **Perspectives**: Challenging you to look at the topic from alternative angles.
5. **Implications**: Exploring the logical consequences of what you say.
6. **Meta-questions**: Reflecting on the discussion itself and how your thinking has evolved.

## How to Trigger

Ask your AI agent tasks like:

```text
Teach me about photosynthesis using the Socratic method.
```

```text
Help me deeply understand how blockchain works.
```

```text
I want you to act as Gadfly and question my understanding of quantum physics.
```
